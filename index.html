About

This application is designed to help users effectively track and manage their mental health. With a variety of psychological tools and techniques, you will be able to better understand your emotions and find appropriate ways to deal with the problems that arise.

Frequently Asked Questions

A collection of common questions and answers that customers, clients or users often ask, a useful resource that provides quick and easy access to information about our app.

What is this app for ?

This app is designed to provide a safe and confidential space for individuals to express their thoughts and feelings. Our AI chatbot uses advanced algorithms to analyze your conversations and assess your mental health. Based on this assessment, you will receive personalized recommendations and resources.

How does the AI work ?

Our AI chatbot is trained on a massive dataset of conversations and mental health information. When you chat with the bot, it analyzes your words, phrases, and overall tone to identify potential signs of emotional distress or mental health concerns.

Is my data private and secure ?

No, our AI chatbot is not a replacement for a mental health professional. While it can provide support and guidance, it is important to consult with a therapist for a comprehensive evaluation and treatment plan.
